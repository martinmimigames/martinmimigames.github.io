android car 3

Based on "car game (java)", which is a port of "car game",

android car 3 is the final form of the porting of "car game (java)."

Using android's canvas methods, and non-threaded,

it is pretty unoptimised.

Due to graphical needs, martinmimi developed android car 4,

which uses opengl, and therefore development for android car 3 had stopped.
